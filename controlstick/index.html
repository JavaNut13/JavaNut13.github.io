ControlStick BETA

ControlStick allows you to play any game with a controller by emulating a keyboard and mouse. This means you can play your favorite games from the comfort of your couch!

Download!

You can grab ControlStick here. You'll need Java for this to run.

ControlStick also needs a wee native library to run, download this and keep it in the same folder as the JAR file!
